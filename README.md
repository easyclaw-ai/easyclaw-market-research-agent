# EasyClaw Market Research Agent

Open-source AI market research and competitor monitoring workflow automation platform for market intelligence and trend tracking.

## What is this

EasyClaw Market Research Agent is an AI-powered workflow automation system designed for:

- Competitor monitoring
- AI market research
- Trend analysis
- Product tracking
- Market intelligence automation

It helps teams automate repetitive research workflows using browser automation and AI agents.

---

## Features

- Competitor tracking
- AI market research automation
- Website monitoring
- Pricing analysis
- Product launch monitoring
- AI summaries
- Browser automation workflows

npm run start
